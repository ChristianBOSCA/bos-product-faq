How to use this tool

1. Search any product name or SKU — typos are fine — and pick it from the list.

2. You'll see its variants/SKUs, shipping weights, and manual links.

3. Hit + Log question to add a question. If it's specific to an attachment, the tool offers to tag it to that exact SKU.

4. New questions land in Unanswered. Anyone who knows the answer types it in — it moves to Pending approval with their initials and the date.

5. A team lead ticks Approve to verify it (moves to Approved). Editing an approved answer sends it back for re-approval.

Tip: your name is remembered on this device. Use “not you?” in the top corner to switch.
