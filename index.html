How to use this tool

1. Search any product name or SKU — typos are fine — and pick it. The product name links out to its live page on the site.

2. You'll see its variants/SKUs, weights, and manual links. The list opens on All variants (the whole product); click any SKU to see only that variant's questions.

3. Where a question lands — whole product vs. one SKU: with All variants selected, + Log question files it under the whole product (the "umbrella"), so it shows for every variant. Click a specific SKU first and the question is tagged to just that variant. If the tool detects an attachment-specific question, it'll also offer to scope it for you.

4. New questions land in Unanswered. Anyone who knows the answer types it in — it moves to Pending approval with their initials and the date.

5. A team lead ticks Approve to verify it (moves to Approved). Editing an approved answer sends it back for re-approval.

Tip: your name is remembered on this device. Use “not you?” in the top corner to switch. Use the General & Policies entry for shipping/returns/process questions that aren't product-specific.
